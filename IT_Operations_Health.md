# IT Operations & System Health

## Overview
A suite of utilities focused on maintaining administrative efficiency, secure access controls, and the overall stability of the n8n automation engine.

## Solutions Included

### 1. Automated Asset Assignment
Coordinates the assignment of IT equipment and automatically generates accountability documents via Docusign.
- **Workflow**: `AssetOps Slack Admin Only`
- **Result**: Self-service asset tracking with legally binding signatures.

### 2. Gatekeeper (JIT Access)
A Slack-based Just-In-Time (JIT) access system for Okta and Google Workspace.
- **Workflow**: `Gatekeeper - Slack App JIT Access System`
- **Result**: Reduces permanent privileged access, following the principle of Least Privilege.

### 3. n8n System Health Monitor
Automatically identifies and reports workflows that are queued or "stuck" in a running state for excessive hours.
- **Workflow**: `Stuck Workflow in Guesty n8n`
- **Result**: proactive alerts to IT teams before system bottlenecks affect end-users.
